Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this e-commerce site is to give a customer a chance to browse different kind of perfumes. It will let them read the description of each products and compare them.Moreover they will have an opportunity to add it to the card and make a payment after they have confirmed their order.

Audience

We will provide hundreds of perfumes to choose from with different types of poeple. We have a perfume for ages ranging from teenagers to elderly people. We will have a special perfume sets for couples who are about to get married or recently wed with discounts and presents. We will make the perfume packaging suitable for any occasions such as anniversity,birthday and so on if our customers request us.
